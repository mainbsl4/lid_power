About Us

WHO WE ARE

LID POWER SOLUTIONS LIMITED has been incorporated in November 2011. This Company concentrated mainly on Power Solutions, Data Center Turnkey Solutions, building security system and Industrial solutions. More investment for incorporate new brands to fulfill its mission to be a leader in the country. As a result the Company now represents: All Power Products, Total Power and Industrial Solutions, UPS & Harmonic Equalizer, Stabilizer, Precision Air Condition , Mission Critical Monitoring & Management System, Water Detection & related Accessories, Interface, Monitoring & Management, ISEP Remote Alert System, Fire Extinguishing System etc. to provide our valued customer "One Stop Total Energy Solution" in Bangladesh. We have 63+ Employee . From these we have 27 dedicated Technical persons for UPS and Power Products service-support. We have Two Branches and a corporate office and 08 number divisional service point for providing better support to our valued client. Our Management are highly educated, experienced and well known in the Market.
